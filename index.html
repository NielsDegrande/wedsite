practical info

Our wedding weekend will take place in Jamshedpur on October 24–25, 2025.

Please plan to arrive by midday on October 24th so that you can settle in before the Mehendi ceremony.
More details on flights, hotels, and transportation are below.

program

day 1

mehendi ceremony (14:00–16:00)

  • Mehndi, otherwise known as henna, is a paste associated with positive spirits and good luck.
  • We invite you to get your hands decorated with this immersive Indian tradition.
  • Attire: Celebratory colours - flowy pants, dresses, or something simple! You choose :)

sangeet & cocktail night (18:00–22:00)

  • A celebratory evening of music and dance.
  • Attire: festive or semi-formal.

day 2

haldi ceremony (10:00–11:00)

  • Haldi, Holi and Games fun outdoor activities.
  • There will be flowers and colour involved.
  • Attire: Casual, comfortable outdoor wear. Preferably light colours that you don't mind getting messy in. They might get spoiled during the event. Bright, easy-to-wash clothes.

wedding ceremony (15:00–17:00)

  • You are invited to witness a modified Indian wedding ceremony.
  • There will be fire involved.
  • Attire: traditional Indian or formal Western.

reception & dinner (18:00–21:00)

  • You are invited to join us for an intimate dinner under the stars.
  • A toast to celebrating Niels & Prianka and kickstarting an time together.
  • Attire: Anything you like.
